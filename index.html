Where is the Yōkai ?

Find the hidden creature in each level !

Play
  • The game consists of 50 levels with increasing difficulty.
  • The further you progress, the more yōkai appear (up to 60 different ones!).
  • The yōkai to find changes each game, even if you replay the same level.
  • The position of the yōkai is randomized every time.
  • You have 3 chances per level to click on the correct yōkai.
  • If you fail... you start again from level 1.
